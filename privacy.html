Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Roster Industries ("we", "us", or "our") operates several Discord bots, including Kora and Neurox. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our services and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Data Collection

We collect limited information to provide and improve our services:

  • User IDs and Usernames: To identify users for ticketing, commissions, and configuration management.
  • Guild (Server) IDs: To store server-specific configurations, knowledge bases, and logs.
  • Message Content:
    • When you mention our bots or use specific commands, the message content is processed to generate an AI response.
    • If moderation logging is enabled, Neurox or Kora may log deleted or edited messages to a designated channel in your server.
  • Configuration Data: Settings for the Neurox Dashboard, including system prompts and knowledge base files.

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries using Artificial Intelligence.
  • Provide server management tools and ticketing systems.
  • Facilitate custom configurations via the Neurox Dashboard.
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our AI assistants.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Configurations, ticket metadata, and knowledge base files are stored on our secure servers.
  • Retention: We retain data for as long as your service is active or as needed to provide services. You can request data deletion by contacting us.

4. Disclosure of Data to Third Parties

  • AI Processing: Message content sent to our bots is processed by the Google Gemini API. This data is used solely to generate a response.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data.

6. Your Rights

Under certain data protection laws, you have rights including:

  • The right to access your data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via our Discord server.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rosterindustries1@gmail.com.
